Welcome to my page about
m y 90 s PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography in my family has been important. My grandad had a passion to photography in his youth too,
and I had keen interest since childhood too. I usually burrowed without telling my grandad's point and shoot family camera in late 1990s.
Until one day he realised that he always gets some random cat&flower photos developed without his acknowledgment these photos are taken,
he got me my own first point and shoot camera. Which I took with me to my summer camps, photographed my family holidays and especially
I photographed my cats and dogs, and garden. In 2000s I photographed my school activities and school mates. Now we love and laugh at these images.
Eventually I developed serious interest to photography, that I studied BA in Photography at Falmouth University.

My most photographed subjects

1. My pets & other animals.

In my childhood I photographed my cats a lot. Also dogs, but I was such a cat person and I loved my cats so much. In this photo can see their relationship was unexisting. Their names were Fluffy and Prince.

2. My family moments.

When my brother was younger, he loved to be photographer. Now not so much. But he also took my camera without asking me and I got mad at him that he used my film for his silly guy friends.

3. My daily life.

Also I photographed a lot of things happening around me. I was shy and quiet child, and taking photos was perfect way to be social but hide. In my youth I noticed the same that camera helped me as an introvert to hide yet be out with people.

4. Miscellaneous.

My art, my school moments and garden were also themes I photographed. Nature since my childhood has been my playground, and I am still very much connected to nature. I had a dream to become a flower photographer, which sounds silly, but I wanted to make a photo book of flowers.
